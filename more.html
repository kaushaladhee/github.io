My Personal Information

I was born on October 15, 2006 in Gauradha, Jhapa. My family later shifted to Sundarharaincha-4, Morang, which was previously called Sundardulari.

About My Family

My father, Lekhnath Adhikari, and my mother, Sita Adhikari, raised me in a lower-middle-class family. My grandfather passed away when my father was 8, leaving him to be raised by a single mother along with two siblings: an elder sister and a younger brother.

My father’s elder sister married Mr. Khatiwada, and my father married my mother at the age of 19. A year later, my elder brother, Kailash Adhikari, was born on January 7, 2004.

Life Journey

After my brother’s birth, my parents shifted to Itahari and started a small electric parts shop with an initial investment of about 50,000 NPR, guided by my father’s brother-in-law.

I was born on October 15, 2006, and started school at the age of 3. I completed my Nursery to Secondary education at Tender Feet Boarding School. Due to the unavailability of high school facilities there, I joined Kasturi College, Itahari, where I am currently studying in Class 12.
